Aunty Kiss Kiss

Karen Saniamo

A person who made us love, forgive, embrace, conquer and dream what we once thought was impossible...

We miss you more than you know

One of Her Favourite tunes...
The Temptations - Never Been To Me
One of Her Chill Activities...
Doing crosswords and reading newspapers.
One of Her Outstanding Virtues...
Kindness.
One of Her Dreams...
To travel the World.
One of Her Cool looks...
Her wearing her infamous Reggae Hat, nothing else made her happier.

She is to be remembered not only as one who loved sports in her schooling days as she participated in every game in the school. She is also to be remebered as the most hard-working and resilient worker, as she worked the same position of flight attendant in the Kenyan National carrier, KQ for more than 25 years.


You left us way too soon, but we understand that you are resting and have accomplished the purpose that God gave you

We miss you so much that it hurts...
They say that memories overlook what we try to forget, that is all we have to cherish nowadays, memories...
You were truly a blessing and a pleasure to be in our lives,
You were the kindest, most humble, shy to some while quite cheeky and outgoing to others, funny, honest, caring, empathetic and your grit was out of this world.
You loved, you sacrificed, you loved, you cheered and you helped.

We miss you so much, it hurts...
Will the pain ever end?

‘Accept and Forgive!’
memorable piece of advice that has stuck up until this day.
‘The Television is just meant to make us not to look poor when people come over.’
memorable answer she gave me when she knew that we were doing just fine without the television. It sounded funny at that time, up until we discovered the National Geographics Channel.
‘Happy Sabbath!’
memorable words she would tell us each Friday evening as we ushered the sabbath.
‘I love You!’
memorable words that we hold on to.

Where do broken hearts go?
We miss you so much that it hurts...

Memories overlook what we try to forget,
but can we overlook, can we forget?
Saudade.
